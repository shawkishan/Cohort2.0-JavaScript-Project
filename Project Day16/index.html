GRIPNR introduces a new organized system for Tabletop RPGs beginning with our first 5e campaign, The Glimmering. Whether you’re a n00b or a seasoned pro, The Glimmering has an adventure waiting for you!

Buy a playable hero, find a game, fight your way through adventures, win treasure, and level up as you explore the world of Asuwa and The Moonstone Empire.

Best of all, your game outcomes and progress are recorded on our play platform so your next adventure begins right where you left off.
